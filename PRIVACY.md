# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Personal API Platform (the "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** When you register, we collect information such as your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
- **Usage Data:** We collect metadata regarding your API usage, including IP addresses, timestamps, request rates, agent types, and endpoints accessed.
- **API Keys:** We store encrypted/hashed versions of your API keys to authenticate your requests.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
- To monitor usage and ensure the security, integrity, and performance of the API (e.g., rate limiting, abuse prevention).
- To communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and support matters.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers, database services).
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

## 4. Data Security
We implement physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your personal information and API usage logs for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Services and comply with legal obligations. Revoled API keys are maintained as records but permanently disabled.

## 6. Your Rights
Depending on your legal jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account and revoke API keys directly from your dashboard. For full account deletion, please contact support.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
